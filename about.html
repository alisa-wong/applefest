The 36th Annual Downtown
Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival

Presented by Tompkins Trust Company

Source: Downtown Ithaca

Fun, Food, and Apples.

Ithaca's premier event of the fall, Apple Harvest Festival is a three-day celebration of apples, featuring local produce, fresh baked goods, regional wines & ciders, and live performances. Come explore Ithaca Commons, the historical outdoor shopping mall, while indulging in local goods and finding entertainment for all ages. The Apple Festival is an exciting, and free, way to kick off the harvest season!
